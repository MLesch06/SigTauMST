About Sigma Tau Gamma

Our Mission

Sigma Tau Gamma is committed to building noble men of integrity, leadership, and excellence. Our fraternity's core values—Leadership, Learning, Integrity, Excellence, Citizenship, and Brotherhood—serve as the foundation for our brotherhood and our commitment to making a positive impact on the world.

Our History

Founded in 1920, Sigma Tau Gamma has a proud tradition of developing young men into leaders and role models. Our chapter at Missouri S&T is dedicated to carrying on this legacy by fostering a community built on mutual respect, support, and personal growth.

Core Values

  • Leadership: Encouraging brothers to be leaders in their communities, classrooms, and careers.
  • Learning: Fostering academic excellence and lifelong learning.
  • Integrity: Holding ourselves and each other to the highest standards of honesty and accountability.
  • Excellence: Striving for greatness in everything we do, from academics to community service.
  • Citizenship: Actively contributing to society through community service and positive engagement.
  • Brotherhood: Building lifelong bonds of friendship, support, and camaraderie.
